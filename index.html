About Me

"Only in silence the word, only in dark the light, only in dying life: bright the hawk's flight on the empty sky" -Ursula K. Leguin, "A Wizard of Earthsea"
This is a quote from my favorite book. It speaks on balance. I belive that balance is the most crucial part of a persons life and as I get older the more I understand and appreciate this.
I am 31 years old and I live in Austin, Tx. I have spent the last 10 years playing drums and working for Little Guys Movers, a furniture moving company. I have been lucky enough to travel all over the world playing music. 3 favorite bands: The Only Ones, Black Sabbath, Pulp 3 favorite movies: The Princess Bride, Toy Story, E.T. 3 favorite books: A Wizard of Earthsea, Dune, The Stars My Destination
